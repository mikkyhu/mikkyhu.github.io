Michael Hu

I'm a senior in the Princeton Computer Science department. I work on reinforcement learning and natural language processing in the Princeton NLP lab.

For my senior thesis, I'm very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Tom Griffiths and Prof. Karthik Narasimhan. Previously, I worked with Dr. Anton Petrov and Prof. Loren Dean Williams at Georgia Tech to visualize ribosomal RNA structures.

I'm interested in using language to make machine learning agents more robust and scalable.
